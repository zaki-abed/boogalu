Overview

Boogaluu is a web and app-based digital platform for dance enthusiasts that give them the freedom to learn from a wide variety of dance styles from their favorite dance masters through structured modules anytime & anywhere.

At boogaluu, we aspire to bridge the gap between physical and digital learning through our immersive technologies.

Boogaluu’s learning modules are custom designed by renowned dancers and choreographers to cater to dance enthusiasts at various levels from kids, Adults - Beginners to Advanced. Our objective is to make learning dance fun and easy.

Boogaluu brings a unique opportunity to all creative hearts to create, share, showcase and compete at a global level through our events.

With Boogaluu one doesn’t have to worry anymore about hunting dance studios, traveling to learn from favorite dancers, expensive workshops, and Juggling with your daily schedule.

VISION: To Democratize structured learning using futuristic digital technologies and create a community of creative hearts.

MISSION: Create a digital platform that gives universal access to learn performing arts with structured content curated by the best instructors globally; provide freedom to showcase your talent and opportunity to grow as a community.
